Quarantine

Quarantine is used to keep a person exposed to covid – 19 away form others. If a person is infected with a virus or is suspected of being infected, quarantine can help prevent the spread of the disease.

Instruction to be followed by someone who is quarantine

  • Must stay at home.
  • A person with covid – 19 must stay home for 14 days after the last contact.
  • Should be separated from others.
  • Health should be monitored regularly.
  • Follow the instructions of the state or local health department.

Options considered to stop quarantine are:

  • After day 10 without testing
  • After day 7 after receiving a negative test result

However, the quarantine must continue for 14 days.

Benefits to society from quarantine

  • Minimizes the spread of the virus to society
  • Symptoms are easier to diagnose
  • Associates are easy to identify
  • Associations are minimal
